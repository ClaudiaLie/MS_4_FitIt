Body Building

CrossFit

CrossFit aims to develop fitness in the 10 components of physical training: cardiovascular/respiratory endurance, stamina, strength, flexibility, power, speed, coordination, agility, balance, accuracy.
Our high intensity interval training is coached by professional certificated trainers who will create a safe environment for beginners and follow all the partecipants' journey to their goals.

Trainers: John, Kim.

Muscle Building

Bodybuilding is the use of progressive resistance exercise to control and develop one's muscles (muscle building) by muscle hypertrophy for aesthetic purposes.
Our gyms are equipped with the very latest tools and machines to help you in your Muscles Building training.
Each subscriber will have a first evaluation by a Personal Trainer who will provide a form with exercizes and nutrition suggestions.

Trainers: Hasan, Alex, Luisa.

Pilates

Pilates improves flexibility, builds strength and develops control and endurance in the entire body.
It puts emphasis on alignment, breathing, developing a strong core, and improving coordination and balance.

Trainers: Adriene.
